This stitch divides the shape into two parts by middle line, and then sew from middle line to edge parallelly with a fixed angle. First, we calculate the center point(the black point in Figure 3.13(a) and orientation(the black arrow in Figure3.13(a) of the given shape. Image moment is a certain particular weighted average (moment) of the image pixels' intensities. Simple properties of the image which are found via image moments includes area (or total intensity), its centroid, and information about its orientation.
